The Destination Place on The North Shore

About Us

The waterfront view, the delicious cuisine and the local draft on tap are three of many reasons why Sou'West is a destination in the summer months for Islanders and visitors alike.

Sou'West is located in New London, Prince Edward Island along the breathtaking waters of the New London Bay. One of Prince Edward Island's magnificent waterfront dining locations, Sou'West is sure to please seafood lovers and foodies alike!

Join us for a pint or a seafood dinner. We'll welcome you just the same!
